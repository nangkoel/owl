Module : Procurement
About : Make a Local Purchase Order (PO)
Content :

Steps to make a local purchase order (PO) :

  1. Go to the menu, it will display all Purchaser jobs list (list of PO by company) :

  2. Click total of purchase request by company as above, it will display all the list of purchase requests must be made a purchase order.

    3. Choose data of purchase request (tick Action column) that will be made into purchase order then click the Proses button.

  3. Following the view after click Process button:

  4. Please complete the following details and save.

    5. Purchase Order has been created.

  5. Click icon to view all the local PO list, or searching PO number that was made :

  6. Search result for local PO :

    1. Click icon to edit local PO, on condition of the PO status is Unrelease PO.
    2. Click icon to delete local PO on condition of the PO status is Unrelease PO.
    3. Click icon to choose the signing, the following is a signatory form :

      4. Click signature button, then the signer's name will appear on the PO.

    4. Click icon to view or print the PO in PDF format.
